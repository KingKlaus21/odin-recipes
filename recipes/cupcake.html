Odin Cupcake

An Odin Cupcake

Description

The Odin Cupcake is the last but certainly not least addition to the Odin Dessert collection. The Odin Cupcake puts a unique spin on your traditional cupcake: it is simply better. Don't ask why. How about you try it for yourself and stop asking so many questions.

Ingredients

Dry Ingredients

  • 1 all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar

Wet Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 large eggs, at room temperature
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup hot water

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour, salt, and sugar

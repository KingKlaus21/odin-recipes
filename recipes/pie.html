Odin Blueberry Pie

A Piece of Odin Blueberry Pie

Description

The Odin Pie is the last but certainly not least addition to the Odin Dessert collection. The Odin Blueverry Pie puts a unique spin on your traditional blueberry pie: it is simply better. Don't ask why. How about you try it for yourself and stop asking so many questions.

Ingredients

For Crust:

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into cubes
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • 1/4 to 1/2 cup ice water

For Filling:

  • 5 cups fresh or frozen blueberries
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

For Egg Wash

  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon water

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour, salt, and sugar
  2. Add the cold, cubed butter and use a pastry cutter to work the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs
  3. Gradually add ice water, 1 tablespoon at a time, and mix until the dough comes together
  4. Divide the dough in half, shape each into a disk, wrap in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for 1 hour
  5. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  6. On a lightly floured surface, roll out one of the pie crust disks to fit a 9-inch pie dish. Place it in the dish and trim any excess overhang
  7. In a large bowl, gently toss together the blueberries, sugar, cornstarch, lemon juice, and cinnamon until the blueberries are well-coated
  8. Pour the blueberry mixture into the pie crust
  9. Roll out the second pie crust and place it over the blueberry filling. Trim any excess overhang and crimp the edges to seal the pie
  10. Cut a few slits or create decorative patterns on the top crust to allow steam to escape during baking
  11. In a small bowl, whisk together the egg and water. Brush the top crust with the egg wash for a golden finish
  12. Place the pie onto a baking sheet and bake for 45-50 minutes or until the crust is golden and the filling is bubbly
  13. Remove the pie from the oven, let it cool for 15 minutes, and serve
